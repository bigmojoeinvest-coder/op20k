Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can I get started?
Most clients are fully live within 48 hours of signing up. We handle all the setup — you just need to show up for a 30-minute onboarding call where we connect your phone number and review your workflow. That's it.
What's the $5,000 guarantee, exactly?
Simple. If we don't generate at least $5,000 in recovered revenue (from captured leads, reactivated customers, or new appointments) within 60 days of going live, your first month is free. We track every lead and outcome in your dashboard so the numbers are always transparent.
Do I need to be technical to use this?
Zero tech required. We build everything, connect everything, and test everything. Your job is to answer calls and show up to appointments. The system runs in the background automatically — you just log in to see results.
Will the AI texts sound robotic to my customers?
Not at all. We write your AI messages in your voice — friendly, professional, and on-brand for your business. Customers usually think they're texting with a real receptionist. We'll show you examples on the demo call.
What industries do you work with?
We specialize in local service businesses: dental practices, medical spas, roofing companies, HVAC, home builders, law firms, and similar businesses where missed calls = lost revenue. If customers call or fill out forms to book with you, we can help.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — no long-term contracts. Cancel with 30 days' notice, no penalty, no hassle. We keep clients by delivering results, not by trapping them in contracts.
What if I already have a website or CRM?
We can integrate with most existing tools. The AI system works alongside your current setup, or we can replace pieces that aren't working. We'll assess this on the discovery call and recommend exactly what makes sense for your situation.
Is there a setup fee?
No hidden setup fees. The monthly price is the monthly price. We absorb the setup cost because we're confident in the results — once you see what the system does, you'll stay.
